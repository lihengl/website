Greetings

Hello, and welcome to my website!

I am currently a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University, and will soon be graduating with a Master's degree in Entertainment Technology. I enjoy programming graphical and interactive software applications whose output can be easily appreciated by non-technical users. On the other hand, I like to create cartoonish characters which I hope one day, with my still-accumulating knowledge on the technical side, can be brought to life. The picture above shows one latest example of such interest.

In here you will find a brief description of me, with things that I have spent time on. Be it some text that I have written, some individual work that I have done, or some collaborative projects that I have devoted to with friends. This website is a great medium for me to share them with you.

Please take your time to look around, and feel free to contact me for any comments or questions.

I hope you enjoy your stay!
